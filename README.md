# 🌿 Get Skoned — Dispensary Tycoon

Run your own cannabis dispensary in this fast-paced browser tycoon game inspired by the real **Skones Dispensary**.

Customers walk in every shift looking for something specific — flower, edibles, vapes, concentrates, pre-rolls, or a BSkone handcrafted glass piece. Your job: recommend the right product, nail the upsell, suggest an add-on at the register, and close the sale before they walk out.

Build your reputation, partner with vendors to unlock premium inventory, invest in marketing to grow your foot traffic, and turn Skones into an empire — one customer at a time.

### Features
- 6 product categories including BSkone Bongs 🏺
- Upsell system — push your luck for bigger profits
- Vendor partnerships that unlock premium products
- Marketing campaigns to boost daily customers
- Star review system — happy customers leave 5 stars, unhappy ones leave 1
- Real Skones logo & soundtrack
- Fully playable on mobile 📱

### Running Locally
Double-click `start_game.command` to launch a local server and play with music.
